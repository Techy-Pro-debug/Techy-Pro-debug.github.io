About Masterminds

Providing accessible, anonymous mental health support through technology and human connection

Our Story

Founded in 2025, Masterminds was born from the vision of three enthusiastic individuals: Pradhyot Nirvan, Aarushi Prasad, and Shreeyashi Acharya. With the invaluable support and guidance of our dedicated educators Jatin Thakwani and Pooja Puri, we embarked on a mission to revolutionize mental health support and make it accessible to everyone.

Our journey began with a simple yet powerful belief - that mental health support should be available to all, regardless of circumstances, location, or financial barriers. We recognized the growing need for accessible, compassionate, and professional mental health services in our communities.

Our Future Goals

Looking ahead, we are committed to expanding our reach and impact in the mental health landscape. Our key objectives include:

  • Expand Access: Reach underserved communities and provide mental health support to those who need it most, breaking down geographical and economic barriers.
  • Technology Integration: Develop innovative digital tools and platforms that enhance the therapy experience while maintaining the human connection that is essential to healing.
  • Professional Network: Build a comprehensive network of licensed mental health professionals, ensuring quality care and diverse expertise to meet varied needs.
  • Research & Development: Contribute to mental health research and evidence-based practices that improve treatment outcomes and understanding of mental wellness.
  • Community Building: Foster supportive communities where individuals can connect, share experiences, and support each other's mental health journeys.
  • Education & Awareness: Reduce mental health stigma through education, awareness campaigns, and promoting open conversations about mental wellness.
  • Global Impact: Scale our services internationally, adapting to cultural contexts while maintaining our core mission of accessible mental health support.

Through dedication, innovation, and compassionate care, we strive to create a world where mental health support is not a luxury, but a fundamental right accessible to all.
